About Me


I am a big video game enthusiast, I play music (can even read sheet music!), I draw pixel art, cook food, do math, and- oh, too specific? Anyways, I have created this website to show my current interest. Normally, I would either be playing video games or I will do lots and lots of pixel art. I grew up with sidescrolling games like Mega Man X and Metroid, so majority of the music I enjoy is based them. Oh and RPG's, how can I forget about them? Games like Final Fantasy, then those SRPGs like Fire Emblem is what got me into the game's storyline. Since then, I've been enjoying storylines that involves tragic change of events and... well... feels like watching a soap opera, if I were to be honest. 🤣
